My first picture on the website

This is the first paragraph I have written on a website that I have developed completely on my own. I hope it looks good. I have added no breaks. In the above, you can see a picture stating "Lovely". Lovely is a word used to describe something that one likes. Alternatively, lovely can be used sarcastically to suggest one enjoys something when they really do not. Lovely can be replaced with the following words

  • Great
  • Fantastic
  • FML
  • Fudge

Alternatively, when thinking of other words to replace lovely with, consider the following:

  1. Am I being sarcastic?
  2. Do I want to sound sarcastic even though I am not?
  3. Is what is happening actually lovely?
